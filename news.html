Dishonesty & lack of transparency from the NC State Highway Patrol office

After Amazon's Super Bowl ad promoting AI cameras to find lost pets, we filed public-records requests with NC agencies — including the NC State Highway Patrol — about their partnerships with surveillance vendors like Flock, Amazon and Ring. Mass surveillance in Creedmoor

Flock cameras installed in the small town of Creedmoor feed a network that shares footage with more than 200 police departments — in and out of state — and federal law enforcement, quietly logging the movements of residents and anyone just passing through.
