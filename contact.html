WHO WE ARE

We are privacy advocates

We are North Carolinians and privacy advocates working to protect our local communities from the unregulated expansion of mass surveillance networks — specifically mass surveillance networks from the likes of Flock Safety. Our mission is to educate citizens, promote transparency in policing, and defend our constitutional rights against invasive tracking technologies.

This site is not directly, indirectly, officially, or unofficially affiliated with DeFlock.org. However, we share common goals of privacy, transparency in policing, and respecting the rights of all people living in the United States.

You can reach us at info@deflocknc.org to share information, ask questions, or join the effort to protect our privacy.
