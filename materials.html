HOW TO PRINT
  • SVG files open in any browser — use Print → Save as PDF, or send them straight to a local print/sign shop.
  • Need a raster copy? Open the SVG in your browser and export/screenshot, or import into Canva, Illustrator, Inkscape or Figma.
  • The flyer now includes a scannable QR code linking to deflocknc.org. Need it on its own? Download the QR code (SVG).
PLEASE POST RESPONSIBLY

Put these up where you're allowed to — your own property, community bulletin boards, approved public spaces, and businesses that say yes. DeFlock NC supports peaceful, lawful advocacy only. Don't place materials on private property without permission, and never tamper with or obstruct cameras or other equipment.
